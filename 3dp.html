3D Printing Workshop

7-Day Hands-On Training in Additive Manufacturing

Day 1: Introduction to 3D Printing

Date: 01.05.2025 | Duration: 2 hrs

  • What is 3D Printing? History and evolution
  • Types of 3D printing technologies (FDM, SLA, SLS)
  • Applications in industry, education, and art
  • Overview of workshop objectives

Day 2: 3D Printer Hardware and Setup

Date: 02.05.2025 | Duration: 3 hrs

  • Introduction to FDM printers: Components and mechanics
  • Printer assembly and bed leveling
  • Materials overview: PLA, ABS, PETG, TPU
  • Hands-on: Basic test print

Day 3: 3D Modeling for Printing

Date: 03.05.2025 | Duration: 3 hrs

  • Basics of 3D modeling: CAD software intro (TinkerCAD, Fusion 360)
  • Designing printable objects
  • Understanding tolerances and overhangs
  • Activity: Create and export a keychain model

Day 4: Slicing and Print Settings

Date: 04.05.2025 | Duration: 3 hrs

  • Introduction to slicing software (Cura, PrusaSlicer)
  • Layer height, infill, supports, and speed settings
  • G-code generation and previewing
  • Hands-on: Slice and print a custom phone stand

Day 5: Troubleshooting and Calibration

Date: 05.05.2025 | Duration: 2.5 hrs

  • Common print issues: Warping, stringing, layer shift
  • Printer calibration: E-steps, PID tuning
  • Material-specific print settings
  • Group activity: Diagnose and fix failed prints

Day 6: Creative Projects and Design Review

Date: 06.05.2025 | Duration: 2 hrs

  • Participants work on their own designs
  • Iterative printing and optimization
  • Peer review and feedback
  • Preparation for final showcase

Day 7: Final Project, Review and Certification

Date: 07.05.2025 | Duration: 1.5 hrs

  • Presentation of final 3D printed projects
  • Individual project evaluation
  • Certificate distribution
  • Career opportunities and advanced learning paths
